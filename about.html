My Journey in Dermatology

I understand how frustrating skin and hair problems can be because I have seen it in thousands of patients over the years. I have seen patients lose confidence due to acne scars, pigmentation, hair loss and chronic skin conditions, even though many of these problems are treatable with proper dermatology care.

My journey in dermatology began at Madras Medical College, Chennai, one of the most prestigious medical institutions in India. During my training at Rajiv Gandhi Government Hospital, I mastered complex dermatological conditions and surgical cases, understanding that behind every skin condition is a person seeking confidence.

I established Dr. Alam's Skin Clinic in Erode with a clear goal: to provide structured, evidence-based dermatology treatments and advanced skin surgery under one roof. My approach is simple: listen to the patient, diagnose accurately, and create a structured treatment plan for long-term health.

"We don't just treat skin. We help you feel comfortable in it."
