Acne and Acne Scars Are More Than Just a Skin Problem

If you have acne or acne scars, you may already know how frustrating it can be. Many patients try multiple creams, home remedies and cosmetic treatments but still do not see proper improvement. Acne can leave behind dark marks, scars and uneven skin texture. Acne scars do not improve with creams alone and usually require procedures for improvement.

Dermatologist-Led Acne and Acne Scar Treatment

At Dr. Alam’s Skin Clinic, we understand how acne and acne scars can affect confidence and appearance. Many patients come to us after trying multiple treatments without improvement.

We follow a structured dermatology approach where we first control active acne and then treat acne scars using appropriate procedures depending on scar type.

You don’t need random treatments. You need a proper diagnosis and a structured treatment plan.
