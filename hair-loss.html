The Challenge

Hair Fall Can Be Stressful — But You Don’t Have to Ignore It

If you are seeing more hair on your pillow, in the shower or while combing, you are not alone. Many men and women experience thinning, but most delay treatment until density reduces significantly.

You might have tried oils or clinical shampoos, but hair loss often needs a targeted medical plan to address the root cause.

The earlier you start treatment, the better your chances of controlling hair fall and maintaining your natural hair density.

The Risks

Why You Should Not Ignore Hair Fall

If you delay treatment, hair loss can advance quickly:

  • Hair thinning may increase rapidly
  • Your hairline may recede permanently
  • Hair follicles may eventually become inactive
  • Treatment becomes much more difficult later

Early treatment helps us preserve your existing, healthy hair.

Clinical Advice:

Many topical solutions are ineffective without a proper medical diagnosis. Don't wait until hair density is significantly reduced.

Your Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does my treatment take to show results? +
Hair growth cycles take time. You will usually see a reduction in hair fall within the first few weeks, but visible density improvement often takes 3 to 6 months of consistent treatment.
Is PRP therapy effective for my hair loss? +
PRP is highly effective for improving hair density and slowing down fall in selected patterns of hair loss. We will decide if it's right for you during your consultation.
Can my hair loss be reversed? +
Early-stage hair loss and thinning can often be significantly improved or controlled. Our goal is to stabilize your hair health and boost natural growth.
