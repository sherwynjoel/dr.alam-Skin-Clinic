Treatment FAQ

Most patients require 6 to 8 sessions for significant, long-lasting hair reduction. The exact number depends on your hair type, skin color, and the area being treated.

While many procedures like acne scar removal or mole excision provide permanent results, conditions like pigmentation or hair loss may require maintenance sessions or a specific skincare routine to sustain the outcomes.

Downtime varies. Simple laser treatments might have 2-3 hours of redness, while deeper skin surgery might require 3-5 days of wound care. Dr. Alam will provide a detailed post-care plan for your specific procedure.

You can book directly via our website contact form or click the WhatsApp button for a faster response. Our coordinators will assist you in finding a suitable slot with Dr. Alam.
